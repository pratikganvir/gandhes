About us

GCC is a knowledge hub providing all the possible facilities to build up the personality and character of the student. Environment at GCC ensures the intellectual and personal growth of students. Teachers here strive hard to create an educational environment where knowledge, skills, rich morals and ethical value systems coalesce together to transform greenhorns into competent professionals, ready to take challenges and emerging needs of the society. Regular classroom teaching and counseling throughout the year by committed faculty members and strict enforcement of discipline and code of conduct has led to sustainable academic results in the past 30 years. GCC is indeed ‘Trusted by Students & Tested by Time’.
